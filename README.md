## ğŸ” Analysis & Key Findings ğŸ”
Analysing the Spotify dataset revealed several clear patterns in listener behaviour and track performance. I found Pop and Hip Hop tracks consistently appear as the most popular genres attracting the largest audiences. Songs with tempos between 100 and 130 BPM tend to perform better also suggesting that rhythm and pace play a key role in listener engagement at Spotify. Tracks that are energetic and highly danceable also tend to draw more attention whereas songs with high acousticness are generally less popular. Similarly Pop and Dance tracks have higher valence indicating they sound happier and more uplifting compared to genres such as Metal or Blues. These insights provide Spotify with actionable information to guide playlist creation and marketing strategies allowing Spotify to push content recommendations while understanding what resonates most with their listeners.
